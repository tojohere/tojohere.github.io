Privacy Policy for SentiLog

Last updated: March 26, 2026

SentiLog (the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

1. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data: SentiLog is designed to be a private journal. Most of your data, including journal entries and sentiment analysis results, are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your private journal entries to our servers.

2. Data Storage

All journal data is stored using React Native Async Storage and the local file system on your device. We do not have access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party tools (like AI SDKs) to process text for sentiment analysis. In such cases, only the specific text you provide for analysis may be processed by these services. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at tojothere@gmail.com.
